What are the rules for $\later$?

\begin{mathpar}
  \inferrule
  {\Gamma \vdash V : A}
  {\Gamma \vdash \nxt\, V : \later A}

  \inferrule
  {}
  {\Gamma \vdash \theta : \later A \rightharpoonup A}

  \theta(\nxt x) = \theta(y); \texttt{ret}\, x
